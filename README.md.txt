# EmirCo Coffee Website Project

## Project Overview
This project is a website created from scratch for a fictional business called EmirCo Coffee . The goal of the website is to promote the coffee shop and sell customers coffee. 
---

## Target Audience
The target audience for EmirCo Coffee includes coffee lovers, college students, young professionals, and people looking for a cozy cafe experience. This place can also be a place to get work done and study.

---

# Part 1: Content

## 1. What is the name of your business?
The name of the business is EmirCo Coffee.

## 2. Is the business real or fictional?
fictional.

## 3. What products or services does the business offer?
EmirCo Coffee offers coffee drinks such as espresso beverages, cold brew, pastries, and coffee beans. 

## 4. Who is the target audience?
The target audience includes students, coffee enthusiasts, remote workers, and local community members who enjoy quality coffee and a comfortable environment.

## 5. What problem does this business solve?
This business solves the problem of people wanting high-quality coffee in a relaxing atmosphere

## 6. What makes this business unique compared to competitors?
EmirCo Coffee stands out because it combines handcrafted coffee, modern branding, warm design, and a customer friendly environment

## 7. What pages will your website include?
The website will include the following pages or sections:
- Home
- About
- Menu
- Contact

## 8. Write a short mission statement for the business.
Our mission is to serve high-quality coffee in a welcoming space where people can relax, connect, and enjoy a better cafe experience.

## 9. What tone should the website content have?
The tone of the website should be modern, friendly, and professional.

## 10. What call-to-action do you want visitors to take?
The main call to action is for visitors to explore the menu, want to visit the cafe, and contact the business.

## 11. What information must be immediately visible on the homepage?
The homepage should immediately show the business name, a short tagline, navigation menu, and the call to action button, and a preview of featured products.

## 12. Will the site include images, videos, or other media? Why?
The site will include images of coffee, pastries, and the cafe environment because visuals make the website more engaging and help communicate the brand identity. 

---

# Part 2: Design

## 1. What overall style will the website have?
The website will have a minimalist and modern style. I did not want it to be too over the top to make it easy to navigate 

## 2. What color scheme will you use and why?
The main color scheme will use dark brown, cream, tan, and white. Brown connects to coffee, cream gives warmth, and white helps keep the design clean and readable. I also wanted to keep the colors close in contrast and use minimal amounts of variation to make it easy to look at 

## 3. What fonts will you use for headings and body text?
The website will use Poppins  for headings and Inter for body text. 

## 4. How will spacing and layout improve readability?
The website will use generous spacing, padding, and margins so that sections are easy to distinguish. This makes it easier to skim through and read so customers dont get bored or frustrated looking at it 

## 6. What visual hierarchy will guide the user’s attention?
The user’s attention will be guided with a large hero section, bold headings, buttons, and featured menu cards.

## 7. How will consistency be maintained across pages?
Consistency will be maintained through the repeated use of the same colors and consistent lay out to make it easier to look through and read 

## 8. What icons will you use?
The site will use simple coffee related icons

## 9. What emotions should the design evoke in users?
The design should make users feel relaxed, comfortable, welcomed, and interested in visiting the cafe 

## 10. How will accessibility be considered?
Accessibility will be considered through readable font sizes, and clear navigation 

## 11. What inspiration sources influenced your design?
The design was influenced by modern cafe websites i found on google 

## 12. Describe the layout of the homepage and build the wireframe.
The homepage begins with a header containing the logo and navigation menu. Below that is a hero section with a headline, short description, and button. The next section introduces the business. After that, there is a featured menu section with item cards. The bottom section contains contact information and a simple contact form.
